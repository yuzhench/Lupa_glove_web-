Simulation Engineer

  • San Francisco Bay Area, CA

    High-fidelity simulations are key to bridging the gap between real-world data and deployable robot skills.

    We are seeking a Simulation Engineer to develop and maintain advanced simulations for dexterous manipulation tasks.

  • Responsibilities:
    • Build realistic simulations of human and robotic hands.
    • Integrate visuo-haptic data into simulated environments.
    • Optimize physics for accurate contact dynamics and real-time performance.
    • Support evaluation of AI manipulation policies in simulation.
  • Requirements:
    • 3+ years developing robotics simulations with Mujoco, Isaac Gym, Gazebo, or similar.
    • Expertise in contact modeling and physics engines.
    • Strong programming in Python or C++.
    • Familiarity with domain randomization for sim-to-real transfer.
  • Bonus Qualifications:
    • Simulating tactile interactions.
    • Experience with synthetic data generation.
